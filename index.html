A litte bit about me:

Despite my rather deceptive appearance, I was born in Guadalajara, Mexico but raised in Brownsville, Texas. I have a wide variety of hobbies; starting with more stereotypical ones like video games and ending with more atypical ones like Fire spinng and bartending. I consider myself extremely adapative to any scenario, as well as a quick learner. Why do Web Development?

I've always had a knack for tinkering with various forms of software which is what lead to my interest in computer science as a whole. From there, I found my calling in web development. Watching a site come together bit by bit is an expierence that I could only dream of. I find it fascinating to see how simple text being written in an editor can build something that everyone uses in their day to day life.
